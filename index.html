Chameleon

Chameleons are unique reptiles known for changing color and catching prey with their long, sticky tongues. They mostly live in trees and shrubs.

×

Zebra

Zebras are wild African equines with black and white stripes. Each zebra's stripe pattern is unique, and they live in herds in the savannas.

×

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and legs. They eat leaves from tall trees and live in open woodlands.

×

Monkey

Monkeys are intelligent primates that live in trees or on the ground. They are social animals and can be seen swinging, playing, or grooming each other.
